Troubleshooting Common Errors

"App Not Installed" Error

Solution: You need to uninstall the official Spotify app from the Play Store first. Android doesn't allow two apps with the same package name but different signatures. Go to Settings Apps Spotify Uninstall, then try installing the Mod APK again.

"Login Failed" or "Auth Error"

Solution: Use Email/Password login instead of the Facebook or Google button. Social logins sometimes have compatibility issues with modded apps. If you still face issues, try using a VPN (connect to a different country) for your first login, then you can disable it.

"Grey Screen" or App Crashes

Solution: Clear the app cache and force stop the app. Go to Settings Apps Spotify Mod Storage Clear Cache, then tap Force Stop. Restart the app. If the issue persists, try reinstalling the APK after a fresh uninstall.
